CSS Orrery

Inspired by watching this Kevin Powell video about trigonometry in CSS, I made this pure-CSS orrery. (There's Sass here, does that still count as pure CSS? There's certainly no JavaScript)

Scaling things

In order to fit this on the screen in a way that makes any kind of sense, some scaling choices have had to be made:

metric scaling
planetary radius 3.5x104km 1vw
mean planet-sun distance 108km 1vw
orbital period 365 Earth days 3.65 seconds

In addition, the Sun's radius is scaled by 3x106km to 1vw, putting it completely out-of-whack with the planets

Note: 1vw is 1% of screen-width

If you zoom in, you can see Mercury whipping round at 19 to the dozen
