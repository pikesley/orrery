CSS Orrery

Inspired by watching this Kevin Powell video about trigonometry in CSS, I made this pure-CSS orrery.

Scaling things

In order to fit this on the screen in a way that makes any kind of sense, some scaling choices have had to be made:

metric scaling
planetary radius 3,000km to 1 pixel
mean planet-sun distance 107km to 1 pixel
orbital period 365 Earth days to 3.65 seconds

In addition, the Sun's radius is scaled by 300,000km to 1 pixel
